About

Mary Schepisi has had many exhibitions in Melbourne, New York City, Los Angeles and London, and her work is held in numerous collections.

Known for the duality of her work, she combines her interest in the beauty of nature and form with hard-hitting commentary on the paradox of life and her deeply felt concerns for humanity - not least of these, in recent years, the plight of refugees around the world.

In addition to her own art practice, Schepisi is an active supporter of art philanthropy. In 2009 she conceived and organised an art auction that raised $84,000 for the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.

In 2014 she then conceived and organised New Directions Art Auction, a bi-annual charity event to raise awareness and funds for the Australian Prostate Centre. Funds from the 2021 auction helped the APC purchase outstanding new technology for prostate biopsy – a machine that provides prostate cancer diagnosis using a simpler biopsy method performed in the clinic.
